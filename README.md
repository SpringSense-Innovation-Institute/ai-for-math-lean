# AI for Math — Lean Code Repository

This repository contains Lean code developed across our organization, collected in one place to support our internal work and our broader effort on AI for Math.

## Who We Are

The Shanghai Xuhui SpringSense Innovation Institute was co-founded by Professor Yu Yong, founder of the ACM Honored Class at Shanghai Jiao Tong University, and Mr. Zhang Yiming, founder of ByteDance. SpringSense is dedicated to cultivating top-tier talent in Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence, with a strong emphasis on global perspective, innovative capability, and social responsibility.
Each year, SpringSense recruits 30 outstanding individuals aged 16–18 with relevant disciplinary strengths to join as Full-time Research Trainees. These trainees participate in a comprehensive five-year program that is equipped with premium educational resources and follows a project-driven learning model.
